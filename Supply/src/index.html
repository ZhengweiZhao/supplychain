Supplychain

Brief Introduction:
This system can record every transaction and receipt in the supply chain, and at the same time introduce the bank to confirm the transaction of these information, to ensure the authenticity of the transaction and documents.
It supports 4 basic functions: purchase from the suppliers companies, transfer receipts, financing and payment.
